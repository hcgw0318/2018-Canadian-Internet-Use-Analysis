The terms of use of the odesi portal, found here: https://search1.odesi.ca/#/terms, disallows the reproduction or transmittance of the data obtained from the portal.

Raw data can be obtained through the following steps:
1. Visit the odesi data portal at odesi.ca
2. Browse data available
3. Select "Communications and Information"
=> "CANADA"
=> "Canadian Internet Use Survey (CIUS)"
=> "2018"
=> "Canadian Internet Use Survey, 2018"
4. Click the download button (floppy disk) near the top right of the site
5. Select the CSV download option and login with your organization credential if prompted
